Ways to Support



Because Florida shelters and rescues are oversaturated with animals in need, we have very limited resources to work with. We as foster parents don't recieve any supplies for our fosters. Your support is crucial!

If you live in the Orlando area and are interested in volunteering, please click here!

Donations


We are working on becoming an independent 501(c)(3) rescue. In the meantime, your direct contributions are carefully tracked and allocated, but not tax-deductible. Monetary contributions are used ONLY to purchase supplies for our fosters that we can't get through our Amazon wishlist or to cover emergency veterinary care.
