Rescuing Kittens in Orlando, Florida

In most places, “kitten season” has a beginning (spring) and an end (fall.) In Florida, kitten season is ALL YEAR LONG! In the South, orphaned newborn kittens are plentiful—but foster parents are not.

Our mission is to save as many kittens as possible: by caring for them ourselves; by raising awareness of the issues facing orphaned kittens; and by providing resources (and eliminating barriers!) for others wanting to get involved.
