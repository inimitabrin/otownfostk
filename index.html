Foster Kittens in Orlando, Florida

In most places, “kitten season” has a beginning (spring) and an end (fall.) In Florida, kitten season is ALL YEAR LONG! In the South, orphaned newborn kittens are plentiful—but foster parents are not.

Our mission is to save as many kittens as possible; both by caring for them ourselves, and by raising awareness of the issues facing orphaned kittens.
