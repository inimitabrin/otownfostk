About Us



The Humans



Hi! We're the foster parents behind O-Town Foster Kitties, Ashley and Madison. Madison is on the left and I (Ashley) am on the right. After moving to Orlando, I went to the county shelter to pick out a companion for my kitten Drake. There, I was faced with the stark reality of cat overpopulation in the South. I ended up clearing some space in my apartment to foster. A few months later, Madison did the same!

One day, we hope to live in a house with tile and expand our fostering operation. My plan is to eventually run a rescue for neonatal kittens full-time.
